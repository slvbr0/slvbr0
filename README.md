### What I build

**Discovery radar** — a personal signal-aggregation feed that pulls and weights activity across multiple sources (social signals, subreddit subscriptions) and groups it into lanes, refreshed on an automated schedule. Live at [slvbr0.github.io/radar.html](https://slvbr0.github.io/radar.html).

**LLM cost-routing experiments** — I've been benchmarking whether you actually need a frontier model for most prompts, or whether a fused panel of cheap/open models gets you there for less. Personal result so far: a routed panel of budget models landed within ~1% of a frontier model's output quality at roughly half the inference cost. Still iterating.

**Poker solver (CFR-based)** — a game-theory-optimal solver experiment for heads-up play. Being cleaned up for open-sourcing — not public yet.

More of what I'm building lives at [slvbr0.github.io](https://slvbr0.github.io) (see the Projects page).
